Endourologic approach to transplant kidney.
.P
JOURNAL ARTICLE.
.W
Two significant procedures, namely lithotripsy of staghorn calculus and dilatation of a stenotic calyceal infundibulum, were performed endourologically in a renal allograft drained by an ileal conduit. This is another example of the versatility of these minimally invasive urologic methods.
