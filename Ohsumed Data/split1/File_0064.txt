Centrifugal cytology: a new technique for urine cytology.
.P
JOURNAL ARTICLE.
.W
A new technique, centrifugal cytology, appears to have a high recovery rate of malignant cells from voided urine specimens resulting in a high probability of early disease detection and should be considered a good supplement to cytoscopic evaluation of high-risk populations.
