Diethylstilbestrol in pregnancy: an update.
.P
JOURNAL ARTICLE; REVIEW.
.W
Several decades ago, diethylstilbestrol (DES) was considered efficacious in improving pregnancy outcome. Later data did not support this, and the exposed mothers and offspring have suffered from a variety of problems attributed to the drug. Knowledge of these problems is essential in dealing with these patients.
